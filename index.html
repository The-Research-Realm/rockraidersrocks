Optional Add-ons

  • OpenLRR
    An open-source reimplementation of Lego Rock Raiders. Built for modern systems with support for higher resolutions, improved performance, compatibility enhancements, and a solid foundation for modding. Provides improved stability, input support, and rendering across platforms while retaining the original gameplay logic.
  • Lego Rock Raiders Community Edition
    A refined version of the original game that includes high resolution support, visual and AI improvements, overall performance boosts, bug fixes, and 8 custom training levels that appear in the main menu. Designed for players who want a polished but faithful experience.

Standalone Overhaul

  • Baz's Mod
    A complete overhaul of Lego Rock Raiders offering a much harder and more strategic version of the game. Includes rebalanced mechanics, new enemy behaviors, redesigned levels, and a focus on challenge and replayability. This mod is separate from the base game and is installed as its own standalone version.
